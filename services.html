Some of the complex diseases handled on a daily basis include:

  • Gastroesophageal Reflux Disease
  • Failure to Thrive
  • Chronic Vomiting
  • Acute and Chronic Abdominal Pain
  • Chronic Constipation
  • Crohn’s Disease / Ulcerative Colitis
  • Chronic Diarrhea
  • Celiac Disease
  • Rectal Bleeding
  • Gastrointestinal Polyps
  • Jaundice / Hepatitis
  • Liver Diseases
  • Pancreatic Diseases
  • Short Gut Syndrome
  • Nutritional Disorders
  • Obesity

Procedures performed include:

  • Upper Endoscopy
  • Colonoscopy
  • Capsule Endoscopy
  • Intestinal polypectomy
  • Pancreatic Stimulation Test
  • pH Probe and Bravo placement
  • Breath Hydrogen Tests
  • Variceal Banding
  • Esophageal Dilation
  • Endoscopic Botox and Steroid Injections
  • Percutaneous Liver Biopsy
  • PEG tube placement

Procedures are performed at Advent Health Children’s Hospital, Arnold Palmer Hospital or Millenia Surgery Center.
